This is less of a hardfast rule and more of a general guide.

Tier 1- 10 second respawn.
Pistol - 54 bullets
SMG - 90 bullets

Tier 2 - 20 second respawn
AR2 - 30 bullets
Shotgun - 8 Shells
Grenade - 2 Grenades

Tier 3 - 30 second respawn
357 - 6 bullets
Crossbow - 4 bolts
SLAM - 1 slam

Tier 4 - 60 second respawn
Rocket Launcher - 2 Rockets
